Prior rulings addressed AI in drafting, research, discovery production, and evidence — but none addressed contemporaneous AI use during a live proceeding. While the pro se context limits immediate applicability, this ruling signals that courts will not allow AI to function as an unnamed participant in legal proceedings. |
