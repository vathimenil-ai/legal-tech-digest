### Tier 1 Development

The **Fortis Advisors v. Krafton** Delaware Chancery ruling represents a genuinely new and material development. This is the first tracked instance of AI conversation logs being used as **affirmative evidence against a party in high-stakes commercial litigation** ($250M earnout dispute). Prior case law established discoverability and privilege risks; this ruling demonstrates AI records being **weaponized** as the evidentiary basis for an adverse ruling. This has direct implications for LSP governance services, eDiscovery workflows, and client advisory.
