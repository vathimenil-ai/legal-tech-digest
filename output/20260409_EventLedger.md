| **analyst_note** | Claude Managed Agents is a qualitative escalation in the disintermediation threat. The standing view correctly identified Harvey's Spectre as pressure on orchestration exclusivity from above (AI vendor building autonomous operations). The critical question for LSPs: does your orchestration moat depend on proprietary infrastructure (now under pressure) or on proprietary data, relationships, and domain expertise layered on top of infrastructure (still defensible)?
